We are Kolumbus

Kolumbus is a vagrant creative network aiming to connect digital explorers who love discovering new horizons.

KOLUMBUS came into existence to combine bleeding edge digital disciplines. By blurring the lines between design, coding and adjacent crafts we want to explore the extra mile. At KOLUMBUS we share the belief that the best way to predict the future is to actually create and implement it, now.
